★ What is Task Dawn?

Task Dawn aggregates tasks from Google and iCloud, ranks by urgency, age, and your settings, then emails a top 3 summary every weekday morning—no new apps, just enhanced routine.

How it Works
Task Dawn runs as a background service on your PC. Simply configure your accounts and let it do the rest!

# Step Description
1 Connect Link your Google Tasks & iCloud accounts via IFTTT Bridge
2 Configure Set your priority weights and email schedule
3 Receive Get your Top 3 tasks every weekday morning!

📝 iCloud Support: Uses the 'Cloud-Bridge' method. Users connect iOS Reminders to Google Tasks via IFTTT. This allows Siri commands to flow directly into the Task Dawn engine.

⚙ The 'Ignite' Engine - Detailed Logic:

Tier Condition Priority
Tier 1 Strings containing 'URGENT' ★★★ Highest
Tier 2 Overdue timestamps (past due date) ★★ High
Tier 3 First-In-First-Out / Creation date (oldest first) ★ Normal

Features
✔ Google Tasks & iCloud Integration (via IFTTT Bridge)
Seamlessly pulls tasks from both ecosystems into one unified view.
✔ The 'Ignite' Ranking Algorithm (Urgent > Overdue > Oldest)
Proprietary sorting ensures you always tackle what matters most first.
✔ Zero-Dashboard Experience (Everything happens in your Email)
No new apps to check. No websites to visit. Just your inbox.

✎ The No-App Manifesto
THE TASK DAWN PHILOSOPHY:

Most productivity tools want you to live inside their dashboard. We believe the best interface is no interface.

Task Dawn spends 2 seconds in your inbox and then gets out of your way. No syncing, no logging in, just clarity.
